Join Us

We are looking for graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and research assistants with research background in organic chemistry and material sciences. We will provide full financial support to the successful applicants. Undergraduate students are also welcomed to join us for summer research or/and undergraduate thesis research

Applicants should send their application to Prof. Miao at miaoqian@cuhk.edu.hk.

Graduate Student Application

Applicants for graduate studentship should provide a resume with a transcript and score of English test (TOEFL or IELTS). Please refer to the Graduate School for the requirements and procedures of applying for graduate study.

Excellent candidates will be nominated for the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme

Interested applicants are greatly encouraged to participate in the Summer Camp for Early Admission
